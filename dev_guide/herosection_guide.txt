This guide details the hero section design for MaskBar.pk - a sophisticated, organic skincare brand hero that balances elegance with natural beauty elements.

## ðŸŽ¨ Design Analysis

### Layout Structure
- **Split Layout**: Two-panel vertical design (50/50 split)
- **Left Panel**: Text-focused content area
- **Right Panel**: Product showcase with lifestyle imagery
- **Dynamic Border**: Angular white border separating panels for modern visual interest

### Left Panel - Content Area

#### Background & Texture
- **Base**: Clean white background (#FFFFFF)
- **Overlay**: Subtle organic pattern - light beige/pinkish-brown floral/vine graphics
- **Purpose**: Adds organic elegance without distraction
- **Opacity**: Very low (5-10%) to maintain readability

#### Typography Hierarchy
1. **Primary Headline**: "Natural & Organic"
   - Font: Classic serif (Playfair Display)
   - Color: Dark charcoal (#2B2B2B)
   - Weight: 600-700
   - Size: 3rem (--fs-2xl)

2. **Secondary Headline**: "Skin Care"
   - Font: Classic serif (Playfair Display)
   - Color: Dark charcoal (#2B2B2B)
   - Weight: 600-700
   - Size: 3rem (--fs-2xl)

3. **Body Text**: "USE OUR ORGANIC SKINCARE, YOU CAN HAVE BEAUTIFUL, LUMINOUS SKIN WITH JUST A COUPLE OF MINUTES OF EASY, DAILY CARE."
   - Font: Sans-serif (Inter)
   - Color: Dark charcoal (#2B2B2B)
   - Weight: 400
   - Size: 1.125rem (--fs-md)
   - Line Height: 1.6

#### Interactive Elements
- **Search Bar**: 
  - Background: White with rounded corners
  - Placeholder: "Search..." in light gray
  - Border: Subtle border using --color-border
  - Button: Dusty rose/muted reddish-brown with white search icon

### Right Panel - Product Showcase

## ðŸ“± Responsive Behavior

### Desktop (1200px+)
- Split 50/50 layout maintained
- Full product showcase visible
- Search bar prominently displayed

### Tablet (768px - 1199px)
- Maintains split layout with adjusted proportions
- Slightly reduced font sizes
- Product image scales proportionally

### Mobile (320px - 767px)
- Stacked layout (vertical)
- Product showcase faded black with content area and searhcbar over it
- Typography scales down appropriately
