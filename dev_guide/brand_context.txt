MaskBar.pk — by Mehroob
MaskBar is a Sub Brand of Mehroob(A skin care Ecommerce Store)

1. What is Mehroob
Mehroob is a premium Pakistani skincare Retail brand inspired by Urdu and Persian roots — where “Mehr” means kindness/light, and “Roob” means grace or charm.
The brand philosophy centers around graceful, kind skincare — clean, minimal, honest, and results-driven.
It aims to bring authentic, dermatologist-approved skincare products to Pakistan with elegant, luxury aesthetics.
Tone: Calm · Confident · Premium · Minimalist

2. What is MaskBar.pk (Mehroob Project)
MaskBar is a sub-brand/storefront powered by Mehroob — dedicated exclusively to sheet masks.
It acts as a discovery platform for customers to explore, try, and purchase all types of sheet masks (hydrating, brightening, firming, soothing, etc.) from trusted brands curated by Mehroob.
The store is a soft-launch project to build brand awareness, test market demand, and start generating traffic under the Mehroob ecosystem.

3. The Goal
To position Mehroob as a credible and premium skincare brand.
To build traffic and community around an easy-entry category (sheet masks).
To create a minimalist, high-end online experience that reflects authenticity and trust.
Later, to expand this same audience into Mehroob Skincare (the full product line).

4. What the Website is About
The website is an eCommerce platform focused on selling sheet masks only.
It will feature:
Curated mask collections by skin concern
Educational content (mask types, benefits, usage)
Aesthetic imagery that reflects purity, calm, and premium skincare
A subtle “by Mehroob” signature throughout the design
Example tagline ideas:
“Pakistan’s First Sheet Mask Boutique — by Mehroob.”
“All Masks. One Bar.”
“Unveil Radiance — Powered by Mehroob.”

5. Design & UI Description
Design direction: Minimal · Elegant · Calm · Boutique
Layout inspiration: Similar to Skinsho (as shown in the reference screenshots).
Clean white backgrounds with subtle beige or blush undertones
Soft serif + sans-serif font pairing (luxury + modern balance)
Hero banner with product photography and tagline
Grid-based product layout (consistent spacing, no clutter)
Hover effects with subtle animations (scale, shadow, fade)
Use whitespace generously
